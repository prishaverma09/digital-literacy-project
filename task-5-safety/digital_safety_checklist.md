Task 5 Part B: Digital Safety & Security Checklist

As a Student Digital Ambassador at VIT Bhopal I make sure to follow these security steps to keep my school and personal information safe.

 1. Password. Mfa

What I do: I use strong passwords for each account. I mix up symbols, numbers and capital letters.

How I stay secure: I have turned on Multi-Factor Authentication on my VIT Gmail, GitHub and LinkedIn accounts. This helps stop people from getting into my accounts without my permission.

 2. Secure Browsing & Network Safety

What I do: I do not do banking or look at sensitive websites when I am using public Wi-Fi that is not secure.

How I check: I always look for the lock icon and make sure the website address starts with https://. This tells me that the connection is secure.

 3. Device & Software Hygiene

What I do: I keep my computers operating system and antivirus software up to date. This helps fix any security problems.

What I am careful about: I only download software from websites like the ones for Python or GitHub.

  4. Phishing Awareness

What I do: Before I click on a link I check where it really goes.

How I am careful: If I get an email that says I need to do something away or reset my password I am very careful. I do not trust these emails.

 5. Data Backup Strategy

What I do: I regularly back up my school projects and code on cloud services like Google Drive and GitHub and, on hard drives.

 6. Legal & Ethical Compliance

What I do: I respect the work of others by giving them credit and using open-source licenses correctly.

What I know: I try to learn about the Information Technology Act, 2000. This helps me understand what I can and cannot do.
